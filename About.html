Teen Patti Palace

Teen Patti Palace, a fresh take on the classic card game from the Indian Subcontinent. Play live with real players from around the world, anytime, anywhere in a language of your choice.

Features
  • Play online - with your friends
  • Play in your language - choose between English, Hindi, Gujarati and Marathi
  • Lots of Variations - different game modes with more added regularly
  • Gifts - send fun and exciting gifts
  • Chat - send and recieve quick messages from your friends
  • Works smoothly on slow Internet connection. Teen Patti Palace works on any data connection or Wifi. Yes! It works great even on 2G.
